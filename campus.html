About Us

New Covenant Life Campus Fellowship The NCLF shall operate in all tertiary (post secondary school) institutions being supervised by a patron or matron who is a staff of a department in the campus. Aims And Objectives a. To impact and instill into the students SPIRITUAL AND A NEW SOCIAL ODER through a compassionate, transformational and exemplary leadership based on the application of principles of the word of god via cooperate leadership training, business and family seminars and welfare schemes. b. Restoring men/women to their original position of covenant dominion through the doing and the teaching of the word of faith. These shall be achieved through Radio/TV Broadcasts, Books, Social media and seminars, church, school and medical missionary outreaches.

New Covenant Leadership Bible School

In the New Covenant Leadership Bible school (NCLBS), the word of God is taught with clear understanding that the entire bible has one central message according to the book of Luke chapter 24:27; that Jesus Christ is not just the messenger of God but the message.

.
  1. At NCLBS, the word of God is taught contextually so as to bring out the original intent and meaning of the texts as presented by the author through the help of the Holy Spirit.
  2. At NCLBS, we also believe and teach the prophetic content and context of God’s word according to 2Peter 1:19-21.
  3. The NCLBs is a NEW TESTAMENT LEADERSHIP bible academy that brings the word of faith to its present day power and relevance which is conspicuously lacking in mere theology.
  4. At NCLBS, the Holy Spirit is REVEALING CHRIST AND EMPOWERING THE SAINTS FOR EXPLOITS and there are over 40 causes including marriage school that will advance, add value and impact leadership, grace and power to your life and ministry.

  7. Success in Life and Ministry

  9. Ministry Ethics

  11. Communion with God
  12. Evangelism and Passion for Souls
